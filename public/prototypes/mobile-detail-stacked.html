What this prototype addresses

Problem
Detail modal on mobile is a scrolling wall; tabs (other prototype) fragment context; can we do better with accordions?
Approach
One scrollable page; sections are collapsible accordions; most-important (Status + Body) expanded by default; Activity + Discussion start collapsed but show counts in their headers
Key difference
No tab switching — everything on one scroll. Better for scanning; worse for deep-focus on one section
New: accordion sections replace 3-column split; no tabs required; section headers carry badge counts
Approach comparison
Tabs (other proto) Stacked (this proto)
Navigation Tap tab to switch context Scroll to section
Scanning Must switch tabs to compare All content in one scroll
Focus Clean, one section at a time Visual noise from other sections
Urgency Tab badges hint at open items Section headers show counts inline
Familiar Tab metaphor — widely understood Accordion — less common in admin tools
